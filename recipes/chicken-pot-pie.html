Chicken Pot Pie

Description

One of our most popular and top-rated meals of all time, this Classic Chicken Pot Pie has a flaky, buttery crust, a creamy sauce, and a hearty mix of chicken and vegetables your family will love.

Ingredients

  • Crust
  • Butter or margarine
  • Chopped onion
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chicken broth
  • Milk
  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Frozen mixed vegetables

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan and add onion and garlic
  3. Stir in flour, salt and pepper, followed by broth and milk
  4. Stir in chicken and vegetables
  5. Bake for 30 or 40 minutes
