Greek Salad

Description

This easy Greek salad recipe is our new favorite summer side dish! Just 7 ingredients + a simple Greek salad dressing make it refreshing & delicious.

Ingredients

  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Olives
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Feta Cheese
  • Olive Oil
  • Red Winegar
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Dijon Mustart
  • Salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Make the dressing: whisk together the olive oil, vinegar, garlic, oregano, mustard, salt, and several grinds of pepper
  2. Arrange the cucumber, green pepper, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, red onions, and olives.
  3. Drizzle with the dressing and very gently toss.
  4. Sprinkle with a few generous pinches of oregano and top with the mint leaves
  5. Season to taste and serve
